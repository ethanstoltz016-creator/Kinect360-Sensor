# Kinect360-Sensor
Software and Driver installers for Xbox 360 Kinect Sensor, I put them together in here along with instructions for ease of use.

**Turn off Memory Integrity**
1. Press Windows Key
2. Type Security
3. Click on _Windows Security_
4. Click on _Device Security_
5. Under _Core Isolation_, click _Core isolation details_
6. Under _Memory Integrity_ click the switch so that it is off
7. Restart your device

**Install Kinect for Windows SDK** 
1. In this folder, open the file labeled "KinectSDK-v1.8-Setup.exe"
2. Click "I agree to the license terms and conditions"
3. Click "Install"
4. A box may appear asking for your permission, click "Yes"
5. Once setup completes, click "close"

**Install Kinect for Windows Developer Toolkit** 
1. In this folder, open the file labeled "KinectDeveloperToolkit-v1.8.0-Setup.exe"
2. Click "I agree to the license terms and conditions"
3. Click "Install"
4. A box may appear asking for your permission, click "Yes"
5. Once setup completes, click "close"

To use: 
1. Press Windows Key
2. type kinect
3. open "Developer Toolkit Browser v1.8.0 (Kinect for Windows)"
4. in the navigation bar, click "Samples: C#"
5. to open the main controller, click "run" under "Kinect Explorer-WPF".
6. to open the camera only, click "run" under "Color Basics-WPF".
7. to open the depth camera only, click "run" under "Depth Basics-WPF".
8. to open the infrared camera only, click "run" under "Infrared Basics-WPF".
9. to open the skeleton only, click "run" under "Skeleton Basics-WPF".
10. to open the face tracking cam only, click "run" under "Face Tracking Basics-WPF".
